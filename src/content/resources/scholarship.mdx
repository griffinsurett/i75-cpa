## i75 CPA Review Scholarship

We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to pursue their CPA certification, regardless of financial circumstances. The i75 Scholarship Program is designed to help dedicated candidates who need financial assistance to access our comprehensive CPA review materials.

### Eligibility

- Currently enrolled in or recently graduated from an accredited accounting program
- Demonstrated financial need
- Commitment to pursuing CPA certification

### How to Apply

Complete the scholarship application form below. Our team will review your application and respond within 2-3 weeks.
