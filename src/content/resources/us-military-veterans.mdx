## Military Discount Program

At i75 CPA Review, we're proud to support those who have served and continue to serve our country. We offer exclusive discounts to help military members and veterans achieve their CPA certification goals.

### Who Qualifies?

- **Active Duty** - Currently serving in any branch
- **Veterans** - Honorably discharged service members
- **Reserve & National Guard** - Part-time service members
- **Retired Military** - Those who completed their service

### How It Works

1. Fill out the form below with your information
2. Upload a copy of your Military ID or DD-214
3. We'll verify your status within 24-48 hours
4. Receive your exclusive discount code via email

### Thank You for Your Service

Your dedication and sacrifice make our freedom possible. This discount is our small way of saying thank you and supporting your career transition or advancement.
